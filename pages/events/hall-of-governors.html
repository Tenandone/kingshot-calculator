Hall of Governors is a biweekly event in Kingshot that takes place during the first and second generations of a kingdom. The event lasts several days, with different tasks available each day. Completing these tasks earns you points and milestone rewards.

Players can also compete on the points leaderboard to win Amadeus shards during the event.

1st Hall of Governors

The first Hall of Governors event begins on day 6 after the state is created. Amadeus is the Hero of the Season for this event.

Stage 1: City Construction

  • Raise 1 Power through Construction – 45 points
  • Raise 1 Power through Research – 45 points

Stage 2: Hero Development

  • Play Hero Roulette once – 90,000 points
  • Use 1 Rare Hero Shard to ascend Heroes – 4,000 points
  • Use 1 Epic Hero Shard to ascend Heroes – 14,000 points
  • Use 1 Mythic Hero Shard to ascend Heroes – 35,000 points
  • Gather 50 Bread in the Wilderness – 3 points
  • Gather 50 Wood in the Wilderness – 3 points
  • Gather 10 Stone in the Wilderness – 3 points
  • Gather 2 Iron in the Wilderness – 3 points

Stage 3: Train Troops

  • Train 1 Level 1 Troop(s) – 90 points
  • Train 1 Level 2 Troop(s) – 120 points
  • Train 1 Level 3 Troop(s) – 180 points
  • Train 1 Level 4 Troop(s) – 265 points
  • Train 1 Level 5 Troop(s) – 385 points
  • Train 1 Level 6 Troop(s) – 595 points
  • Train 1 Level 7 Troop(s) – 830 points
  • Train 1 Level 8 Troop(s) – 1,130 points
  • Train 1 Level 9 Troop(s) – 1,485 points
  • Train 1 Level 10 Troop(s) – 1,960 points

Stage 4: Beast Slay

  • Kill a Level 1 to Level 30 Beast – 30,000 points
  • Call a rally and hunt down 1 Terror – 90,000 points

Stage 5: Power Boost

  • Raise 1 Power through Construction – 30 points
  • Raise 1 Power through Research – 30 points
  • Raise 1 Power by training/promoting troops – 20 points
