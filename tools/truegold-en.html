What is Tempered Truegold?

A special material required after TG5 for building upgrades. It is Truegold refined one more step into a higher-grade resource.

When do you need it?

  • Before TG5: Upgrades require Truegold + standard resources only
  • After TG5: Upgrade requirements include Tempered Truegold
  • TG8: Typically unlocks around Day 310–320 (server age)

Material overview

Truegold is refined in the Royal Foundry to enhance hardness and ductility, becoming an exceptional high-end material.

How to obtain

  • Ultra Refining (Truegold Refinery) — main source
  • Golden Glaive event — supplementary source

Ultra Refining key points

  • Up to 100 times per week, but your weekly total increases tier and makes Truegold cost spike
  • Designed to discourage “doing it all at once” → tier management = efficiency
  • Daily first attempt costs 50% less Truegold (very important)

Ultra Refining tiers (weekly count)

Tier 1(1–20)20 Truegold → 1–3 Tempered
Tier 2(21–40)50 Truegold → 2–3 Tempered
Tier 3(41–60)100 Truegold → 3–6 Tempered
Tier 4(61–80)130 Truegold → 3–9 Tempered
Tier 5(81–100)160 Truegold → 3–12 Tempered

Example: You stay in Tier 1 up to 20 weekly attempts → Tier 2 starts from 21.

Recommended strategy (F2P / low spender)

  1. Monday: do up to 20 attempts first (maximize Tier 1 efficiency)
  2. After that: do only 1 attempt per day (keep collecting the daily 50% discount)
  3. Keep the week near early Tier 2 to earn about 2–3 Tempered/day consistently
Bottom line: If you rush all 100 attempts at once, tiers rise fast and your Truegold cost explodes. For F2P/low spenders, staying around Tier 1 → early Tier 2 is the key.

Practical checklist

  • Monday: use the Tier 1 efficiency window (up to 20)
  • After: do 1/day to keep the 50% daily discount
  • Before upgrading: calculate your total Tempered Truegold requirement first
  • Warning: the more you “bulk refine,” the higher the weekly tier → higher costs later
