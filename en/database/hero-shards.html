Hero Shards

Hero Shards are used to increase a hero’s star level in Kingshot. Star upgrades unlock new skills, improve existing skills, increase power, and unlock the hero story.

This page covers hero shard types, hero rarity, exception heroes, sources, and total shard requirements.

Overview

Note: A total of 1,065 Hero Shards are needed to reach max star level. Including hero unlock shards, the normal full total becomes 1,075.

Hero Shards are pieces used to raise a hero’s star level. When a hero’s stars increase, new skills can unlock, existing skills can be upgraded, power increases, and the hero story opens.

Types

General shards and hero-specific shards are used differently.

General Hero Shards

Can be used or exchanged for most heroes as long as the rarity matches.

Hero-Specific Shards

Can only be used for the hero they belong to.

Exception: Amadeus and Helga can only upgrade stars with their VIP package exclusive shards. General Mythic Hero Shards cannot be used for them.

All other heroes can upgrade stars using general shards.

Hero Rarity

R (Rare / Blue), SR, SSR
