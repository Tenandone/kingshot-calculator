Widgets

Widgets are parts needed to unlock and upgrade Hero Exclusive Gear in Kingshot. If you plan to use a Mythic Hero for a long time, it is better to decide early and invest toward a level 10 Exclusive Gear.

This page explains what Widgets are, where to get them, how many you need for each upgrade level, and how Full Package pricing is commonly understood in USD.

What are Widgets?

Note: Hero Exclusive Gear is only available for Mythic Heroes and becomes accessible once you reach Town Center level 15.

Widgets are parts needed to unlock and upgrade Hero Exclusive Gear in Kingshot.

These exclusive gears are only available for Mythic Heroes and become accessible once you reach Town Center level 15.

You can use Widgets to upgrade Hero Exclusive Gear through ten different levels, boosting the hero’s power massively and unlocking stronger effects for both Expedition and Beast Hunt content.

Even free-to-play players can eventually equip a level 10 Exclusive Gear if they keep collecting Widgets over time through events and shops. It is best to check your long-term hero roadmap first, then invest your Widgets into heroes you expect to use for a very long time.

Sources

Availability may vary depending on server progress and event rotation.

Buccaneer Bounty Event

One of the main event sources for Widgets. If you prepare well for this event, it can become a strong long-term supply route.

Mystery Shop

Widgets can appear in the Mystery Shop, and they may be available at a discount depending on your shop rotation and currency usage.

Hall of Heroes

In later state generations, Hall of Heroes can become another useful source for Hero Exclusive Gear materials.

Special Events

Limited-time events may include Widgets as milestone or exchange rewards, so event planning matters a lot.

Packs

Paid packs are the fastest way to collect Widgets, especially if you are aiming to finish a level 10 Exclusive Gear more quickly.
