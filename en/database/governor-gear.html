Governor Gear

Governor Gear is a special system that allows you to craft and upgrade powerful equipment. It can be accessed from the profile menu in the top-left corner and unlocks when Town Center reaches level 22.

Governor Gear is one of the core systems for boosting your overall power, and upgrading it requires Satin, Threads, and from Rare tier onward, Artisan's Sketches.

What is Governor Gear?

Note: Governor Gear unlocks at Town Center level 22 and can be crafted and upgraded from the Governor Profile screen.

Governor Gear is a long-term progression system that lets you craft and strengthen powerful equipment, significantly increasing your governor's stats and power over time.

Upgrading uses Satin and Threads, and starting from the Rare tier, Artisan's Sketches are also required.
