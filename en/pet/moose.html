Moose

Moose is a Generation 2 pet in Kingshot that becomes available when your state is around 80 days old. Its Horror Stare skill is a battle ability that lowers enemy squad health in every battle you fight for the next two hours after activating it.

This page summarizes Moose's skill effect, total upgrade materials, and the pet calculator link in one place.

Moose is a Generation 2 battle pet designed to weaken the enemy before and during combat. His skill is useful for players who want stronger performance in repeated fights during the active buff window.

Skill Effect

Horror Stare
The giant Moose stares at the enemy before charging, reducing the enemy squad’s health by 5.00% for 2 hours. (Cooldown: 20h)

Upgrade Preview

Enemy Squad’s Health Down: 1.50% / 2.00% / 2.50% / 3.00% / 3.50% / 4.00% / 5.00%

As Moose advances, the health reduction effect becomes stronger. This makes him a strong support pet for battle-focused players who want to increase pressure on enemy squads over a two-hour combat window.
