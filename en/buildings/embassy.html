Embassy

The Embassy is a key alliance & defense building in Kingshot. You’ll need to upgrade it to progress your city (it becomes required for Town Center upgrades from Level 9+).

“Issues and responds to Alliance Help and reinforcement requests. Upgrade to increase the Embassy’s capacity to receive reinforcements and take more requests.”

What the Embassy upgrades improve

Upgrading the Embassy improves three important stats

  • Ally Assists: How many times alliance members can help speed up your upgrades, research, or troop healing.
  • Ally Help Time: Seconds reduced from an ally’s upgrade/research/healing when you assist them.
  • Reinforce Capacity: Maximum troops you can receive as reinforcements to defend your city.
Note: Resource costs can vary with heroes/research buffs. Values shown here are raw/base values.
