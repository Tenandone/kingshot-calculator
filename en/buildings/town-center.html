Town Center

The Town Center is the core of your city in Kingshot. Upgrading it unlocks new features like buildings, Hero Gear, Governor Gear, Governor Charms, and more.

Once your kingdom reaches the Age of Truegold (often around day 60–70 on many servers), you can upgrade your Town Center past level 30. These are Truegold levels and require Truegold in addition to Bread, Wood, Stone, and Iron.
