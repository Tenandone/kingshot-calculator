Overview

The Storehouse is unlocked at Town Center level 9. Its main purpose is to protect your non-secured resources—resources you gain from raids, gathering, or those stored in your backpack.

Note: Resource costs can vary depending on heroes, research, and buffs. Values shown here are the raw/base numbers.

How Storehouse works

There are two types of resources in the game: secured and non-secured.

  • Secured resources are completely safe and can’t be stolen. You get these from production buildings (Mill, Sawmill, Quarry, Iron Mine) or offline income.
  • Non-secured resources include resources from raids, gathering, or those stored in your backpack. These can be stolen if your base is attacked.

The Storehouse protects non-secured resources up to its protected capacity. Resources within this limit stay safe, while any amount above the Storehouse capacity can be plundered. Upgrading the Storehouse increases protected capacity, giving you better protection.
