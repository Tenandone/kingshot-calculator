Kingshot Giant Bear Reward Guide

Giant Bear is content that can be played once every 48 hours. In other words, you can participate once every 2 days, so if you never miss it for a full month, you can play a total of 15 times.

This guide is calculated based on 175M+ personal damage points, and is organized for easy viewing around Crafting Hammers / Hero Gear Chests / Enhancement Parts / Alliance Coins.

Giant Bear Participation Rule

Giant Bear does not open every day like a standard event. Instead, it is cycle-based content that can be played once every 48 hours.
