Overview

Kingdom of Power is divided into matchmaking, preparation, battle, and field triage phases. This page focuses on a quick summary for the 5-day preparation phase.

Note: Please check the in-game event details first for exact points and rewards. This table is a practical recommendation sheet designed for quick reference during real preparation planning.

  • Day 2: Research, roulette, hero shards, and gathering
  • Day 3: Pet upgrades, roulette, hero shards, charms, and intel tasks
  • Day 4: Training, hero gear upgrades, and gathering
  • Day 5: Mixed focus on construction, research, training, pets, hero gear, governor gear, gathering, and intel tasks
