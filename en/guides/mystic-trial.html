Kingshot Mystic Trial Guide | Unlocks, Ratios, Weekly Zones, and Practical Tips

Mystic Trial is one of the most useful weekly PvE systems for long-term account growth. This guide explains unlock requirements, zone-specific stat sources, troop ratio ideas, raid rewards, shop priorities, and the practical reality that most players eventually stop over-tuning every stage.

Core takeaway

  • Mystic Trial is less about raw troop ownership and more about the stat source used in each zone.
  • At first, testing a different troop ratio per zone can help.
  • Later, most players get tired of constant tweaking and end up running a simple all-purpose ratio like 5:2:3 unless a stage blocks progress.

Remember these first

  • Mystic Trial unlocks at Town Center 19 through the Command Center.
  • Each zone uses a different set of Expedition stats, so the reason you clear one zone easily may not apply to another.
  • Outside Radiant Spire, the game provides Lv. 10 troops, so your bonus sources matter more than raw troop ownership.
  • Stage progress can reset, but your leaderboard standing does not.
  • After clearing stages 1–10, Raid unlocks and becomes a weekly quality-of-life feature.

1) Basic information and unlock requirements

Mystic Trial unlocks after reaching Town Center 19 and can be accessed from the Command Center. On new servers, it usually appears after around two weeks.

  • Some Mystic buildings may still be unavailable if you have not unlocked the relevant system yet.
  • That is because each building uses a different group of Expedition stat sources.
  • Bonus Items, King’s perks, Ministry positions, Alliance bonuses, and Outpost bonuses do not affect Mystic Trial.

Important rule

Mystic Trial uses Expedition stats and Expedition-style combat. It is not Conquest combat and it is not Arena combat.

2) Zone explanation and stat sources

Pet skills activate automatically in Forest of Life and Radiant Spire. You do not need to trigger them manually.

3) Stage reset and Raid feature

Mystic Trial zones can be reset by the game’s schedule. When that happens, your stage progress inside the zone resets, but your leaderboard ranking does not disappear.

  • The reset is generally believed to follow a 7-day cycle.
  • After clearing stages 1 through 10, the Raid feature unlocks.
  • When the zone opens again, you can raid previously cleared stages for repeat rewards.

Why Raid matters

Raid turns Mystic Trial from a pure push-mode into a weekly maintenance source of resources. Once unlocked, it saves time every week.

4) Troop ratio advice

In all zones except Radiant Spire, the game provides Lv. 10 troops. That means troop quality is normalized and your account’s Expedition bonuses matter more.

The AI generally uses a default ratio close to 33 / 33 / 33 for Infantry / Cavalry / Archer, except the 10th stage where the ratio shifts to around 53 / 27 / 20.

Safe starting point

If you are not sure what to use, start with 50 / 20 / 30.

How to edit troop formation without wasting attempts

  1. Open Challenge
  2. Tap Edit Formation
  3. Adjust your ratios
  4. Do not press Deploy or Quick Deploy
  5. Use the top-left back arrow to leave safely

The practical reality

At the beginning, many players enthusiastically split ratios by zone and test everything carefully. But after running Mystic Trial for a while, that becomes very annoying. In practice, many players eventually stop over-managing every zone and just run a stable all-purpose ratio like 5 : 2 : 3, only making a small adjustment when a stage actually blocks progress.

Raid Rewards

Raid rewards scale with your highest cleared stage in each zone. A very simple rule of thumb is that weekly Misty Crystals can be estimated by multiplying your cleared stages by 10.

Why this mode matters long term

Mystic Trial is not just one more PvE mode. It is a weekly source of progression materials that keeps paying you if you keep clearing farther.

6) Trial Shop priorities

The Trial Shop uses Misty Crystals. If you keep pushing your stages, you should be able to buy useful things every week without much trouble.

Shop timing notes

  • Mithril appears after Gen 2 is released.
  • True Gold Dust is added later, around the War Academy unlock period.
  • You can access the shop through the bottom menu or the top-right Mystic Trial shortcut.

Simple buying logic

Buy the things that stay valuable on the account for a long time. For many players, that usually starts with Mithril.

7) Practical player advice

Do not pretend every zone needs perfect micromanagement forever

In theory, yes, every zone can be optimized separately. In reality, once Mystic Trial becomes part of your weekly routine, convenience matters. Most players eventually want a setup that is good enough, easy to repeat, and fast to maintain.

Use specific ratios when stuck, not all the time

Zone-by-zone ratio tuning is most useful when you are pushing new walls. If you are already clearing comfortably, using the same practical ratio over and over is usually fine.

Radiant Spire deserves extra respect

Radiant Spire is the exception because it uses your own troops and a much wider set of account stats. That zone reflects overall account depth much more than the others.

Test seriously when you are progressing. Simplify aggressively when you are farming.

8) Final summary

  • Unlock: TC 19 through Command Center
  • Main idea: each zone uses different Expedition stat sources
  • Safe starting ratio: 50 / 20 / 30
  • Long-term practical ratio: many players eventually default to 5 : 2 : 3 unless a stage needs adjustment
  • Weekly value: stage clears, Raid rewards, and Trial Shop materials add up over time

One-line conclusion

Push carefully at first, learn which zones use which stats, then stop overcomplicating the mode and farm it efficiently every week.
