Overview

This Kingshot SSR Hero Comparison Chart is a stat comparison page built for quickly comparing Infantry, Cavalry, and Archer heroes within the same troop type. Attack and Defense are calculated from hero expedition stats, while HP and Might are calculated from Exclusive Gear stats.

When you are deciding which SSR hero to upgrade first in Kingshot, this page helps you compare same-type heroes with Exclusive Gear included and quickly set your upgrade priority. Because Exclusive Gear enhancement is also reflected, it is useful as a fast comparison tool when planning your growth path.

Note: Attack / Defense are based on hero expedition stats, while HP / Might are based on Exclusive Gear stats. Total Stats is a simple sum of those four values for quick comparison.

FAQ

What does the Kingshot SSR Hero Comparison Chart calculate?

Attack and Defense are calculated from hero expedition stats, while HP and Might are calculated from Exclusive Gear stats. Total Stats is the sum of Attack, Defense, HP, and Might.

Should I only compare heroes within the same troop type?

Yes. This page is designed to compare Infantry, Cavalry, and Archer SSR heroes only within the same troop type.

Does the calculator include Exclusive Gear level?

Yes. Exclusive Gear enhancement from +0 to +10 is included and applied to HP and Might.

Does a higher total stat always mean a better hero?

No. Total Stats is a quick comparison indicator. In actual gameplay, you should also consider skills, battle situations, team composition, and upgrade priority.
