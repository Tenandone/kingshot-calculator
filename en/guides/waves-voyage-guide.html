Wavebound Voyage Guide

The core of this event is to gather as many Premium Tidal Treasures as possible and use them as merge materials for higher-tier treasures.

Wavebound Voyage is a 5-day limited-time event. Older versions of this event focused on Lord Equipment rewards, but the current Wavebound Voyage is now centered on Lord Gem rewards.

Low spenders should focus on steady run management and clean end timing, while high spenders can simply push straight to the 600-run max reward line.

This page covers team management, Nautical Compass usage, treasure drop rates, merge structure, the 196-run finishing route, and the 600-run max reward route.

It is organized in order so even first-time players can follow it easily.

Overview

Wavebound Voyage is an event where you send exploration teams, collect Tidal Treasures, and merge lower-tier treasures into higher-tier ones when needed.

Event update note
Earlier versions of this event mainly rewarded Lord Equipment, but the current Wavebound Voyage now focuses on Lord Gem-related rewards.

Main goal

Accumulate Premium Tidal Treasures, then merge them into Exquisite Tidal Treasures or Majestic Tidal Treasures to secure higher-value materials.

Key rewards

Charm Guides, Charm Designs, and Legendary Universal Hero Shards are the most important rewards to watch in this event.

Quick summary: Common Tidal Treasure is your base material tier, Premium Tidal Treasure is the merge starting point, and Exquisite plus Majestic are the real target tiers.

Rules

1. Sending exploration teams

During the event, exploration runs last 8 hours. When a team returns, it can bring back Tidal Treasures based on drop rates, while some rewards are guaranteed.

2. Opening and merging treasures

Tidal Treasures can be opened directly, or merged into higher-quality treasures if you want to aim for bigger rewards.

3. Extra exploration teams

Each Lord starts with 1 exploration team by default. During the event, you can unlock more teams with Diamonds. The 2nd team costs 2,000 Diamonds, and the 3rd team costs 10,000 Diamonds.

4. Nautical Compass

You can speed up an active exploration with Nautical Compasses. Each Nautical Compass reduces exploration time by 1 hour. You get 1 free per day, and extra ones come from packs that include the item.

5. Total exploration milestones

The current total exploration milestone rewards go all the way up to 600 runs.

6. After the event ends

When the reward claim period ends, any unopened treasures are automatically opened and the rewards are sent to your bag.

7. Compass carryover

Unused Nautical Compasses stay in your bag until the next Wavebound Voyage event begins.

Total exploration milestone rewards

The more runs you send, the more total milestone rewards you can collect. These rewards include experience upgrade parts, Legendary Universal Hero Shards, and gem-related materials.

Treasure merge rules

  • 3 Common Tidal Treasures → 1 Premium Tidal Treasure
  • 3 Premium Tidal Treasures → 1 Exquisite Tidal Treasure or 1 Majestic Tidal Treasure
  • Exquisite Tidal Treasure rate: 75%
  • Majestic Tidal Treasure rate: 25%

Rates

Below are the treasure drop rates from exploration, along with the merge rates for higher-tier treasure results.

Common Tidal Treasure x1 Exploration drop rate: 90.00%
Premium Tidal Treasure x1 Exploration drop rate: 10.00%
Merge 3 Common treasures Premium Tidal Treasure x1: 100.00%

Majestic Tidal Treasure x1 Premium merge rate: 25.00%
Key point: Premium Tidal Treasures have a low direct drop rate, so the real flow is to keep stacking Common Tidal Treasures and merge upward steadily.

How to Run the Event

Since the event lasts 5 full days, the most efficient route is to start from day one and keep your exploration running consistently.

Base flow

From the first day of the event, keep exploring steadily without using compasses and build up your total run count.

Low-spend finish route

Near the end, adjust your total to 196 runs, then send 4 final exploration teams to finish cleanly.

High-spend route

Simply keep pushing toward the 600-run maximum reward milestone.

Basic flow

From day one, do not spend Nautical Compasses right away. Keep your exploration teams running as consistently as possible.

  • Try not to leave exploration slots idle
  • Use the first part of the event to build stable total runs
  • Save compasses and speedups for the final adjustment phase

Low-spend finishing route

For low spenders, the important part is the final run adjustment on the last day.

  • Adjust your exploration count to 196 runs
  • If needed, use Nautical Compasses + speedups to reach 196
  • Then send 4 final exploration teams to finish

Reward claim window

After the event countdown ends, the main thing left is treasure cleanup and reward claiming.

  • There is a 1-day reward claim period after the countdown ends
  • During this time, you can only open treasures and merge them
  • No additional exploration can be started, but already deployed teams remain until they finish
Low-spend core idea: Build steadily from day one, adjust to 196 near the end, then send 4 final teams for a clean finish.

Quick Tips

1. Fewer teams means slower milestone progress

If you only rely on the default starting team, your milestone progress will be slow. If possible, opening up to 4 exploration teams makes the event much smoother.

2. Low spenders should start steady from day one

Keep your exploration running from the first day, then only patch missing runs on the last day. This is usually more efficient than burning compasses too early.

3. Save Nautical Compasses for the final adjustment

Do not use Nautical Compasses randomly. They are much more valuable when used to reach the 196-run target or cover your final gap before the event ends.

4. Do not rush to open Premium Tidal Treasures

Opening Premium Tidal Treasures right away is usually less efficient than saving them for merges into Exquisite Tidal Treasures or Majestic Tidal Treasures.

5. High spenders can simply push to 600

The high-spend route does not need to be complicated. Just keep going until you reach the 600-run maximum reward line.

One-line summary: Low spenders finish by adjusting to 196 runs and sending 4 final teams, while high spenders simply push to the 600-run max reward milestone.
