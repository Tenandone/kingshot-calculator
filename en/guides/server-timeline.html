Kingshot Server Timeline (State Age)

As your state or server ages in Kingshot, new features, heroes, and pets gradually unlock. This guide summarizes the approximate timeline for major content such as Generation 1–6 Heroes, Pets, Age of Truegold, Truegold Crucible, War Academy, and server battle eligibility.

Actual timings can vary depending on your server's progress. In many cases, the difference can be around 10 to 20 days, so use this page as a practical reference rather than an exact fixed schedule.
Overview• Timeline• Related Links• Notes

Overview

This page is a practical Kingshot server timeline guide built to help you understand when important content usually becomes available as your state grows older. It covers hero generation changes, pet releases, Age of Truegold, Truegold Crucible, War Academy, and major PvP milestones such as Kingdom of Power.

The dates below are estimated using server age as a reference. Actual release timing can vary by server, so this guide should be used to plan ahead and prepare, not as an exact guaranteed schedule.

How to estimate State Age: If you have not missed any VIP login days, you can use your VIP login record as a rough estimate. If there are missing days, you can check the top completion date in the Monument achievement section and subtract 6 days to estimate when the server was created.
This post will continue to be updated as the server progresses, and it is planned to cover future TG10 timing as well.

State / Server Timeline

Early Content
  • Generation 1 Heroes
    The starting hero pool available when the server opens.
    0 days
  • Fog is lifted from Plains
    An early map expansion milestone.
    14 days
  • Fog is lifted from Fertile Land
    A key point in world expansion.
    38 days
  • Generation 2 Heroes
    The early meta begins to change around this point.
    40 to 50 days
  • Alliance Resource Exchange
    An alliance support feature that improves cooperative progression.
    45 days
  • First Castle Battle
    A major PvP milestone where server competition starts to intensify.
    54 days
  • Generation 1 Pets
    Gray Wolf, Lynx, and Bison become available in the early pet phase.
    55 to 60 days
  • Age of Truegold
    A major progression shift begins. You can also track related building progression on the Town Center page.
    70 days
Server Battle Content
  • Generation 2 Pets
    Cheetah and Moose become available as the server approaches its first large-scale kingdom competition phase.
    75 to 80 days
  • Kingdom becomes eligible for Kingdom of Power
    This is the point where your state can begin participating in major server battle content.
    70 to 80 days
Mid to Late Content
  • Generation 3 Pets
    Lion and Grizzly Bear mark the next pet growth phase.
    105 to 110 days
  • Generation 3 Heroes
    A major hero generation shift that changes lineups and priorities.
    105 to 120 days
  • Truegold Level 5 and Truegold Crucible
    A key mid-Truegold milestone. The Truegold Crucible becomes one of the main reference points for server progression.
    150 days
  • Artisan’s Vision in Alliance Championship Store & Vikings Vengeance Level 12 to 20
    A later progression milestone tied to special systems and event growth.
    170 days
  • Generation 4 Heroes and Generation 4 Pets
    Giant Rhino and Mighty Bison appear in this phase. Gen 2 Heroes also become available in Hero Recruitment and Intel Missions.
    190 to 200 days
  • War Academy
    An important late-game system tied closely to advanced progression and Truegold research.
    220 days
Late Content
  • Generation 5 Heroes and Generation 5 Pets
    Great Moose and Alpha Black Panther mark another major endgame transition.
    270 to 280 days
  • Truegold Level 8 and Tempered Truegold
    A major late-Truegold milestone for long-term progression planning.
    310 to 320 days
  • Generation 6 Heroes and Generation 6 Pets
    Regal White Lion and Ironclad War Elephant become available as the latest high-end progression phase begins.
    350 to 360 days
