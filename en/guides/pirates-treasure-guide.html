Buccaneer Bounty plays very differently depending on whether you want hero-exclusive upgrade materials or you are saving for skins.

Best Strategy If You Want a Skin

If your goal is a skin, do not rush your chest openings. A better approach is to buy only the basic value packs, save keys across 3 to 4 events, and then push for the skin when your stock is ready.

Corsair Keys

Corsair Keys carry over after the event ends, so you can save them for the next Buccaneer Bounty.

Pearls of Enigma

Once you open chests and turn keys into pearls, leftover pearls will not carry over. They get converted into resources when the event ends.

In simple terms

Skin-focused players are usually better off stacking keys instead of converting them into pearls too early.

If you open chests too soon, your pearls will not stay as pearls after the event ends.
