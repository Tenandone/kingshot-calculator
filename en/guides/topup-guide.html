Kingshot Top Up Guide

Most self top-up failures happen before payment, not during payment.

This guide helps Kingshot players complete LootBar self top-up safely by checking the two most important requirements first: direct Google or Facebook account linking and active 2-step verification.

If your account is linked correctly and your security verification is ready, the actual Kingshot top-up flow is usually simple.

This page is for players who want a safer, simpler, lower-friction way to complete Kingshot self top-up and avoid the most common login errors.

Review the requirements once, follow the screenshot steps, and use the support links only if you get stuck.

Check these requirements before you start

Before opening the Kingshot top-up page, confirm these two conditions first. This removes most of the common login and verification problems.

Important: Keep your own account and personal information secure. Self top-up works best when your login method and security settings are already confirmed before payment.

1. Direct account link

Your Kingshot account must be linked directly to Google or Facebook.

2. Security verification ready

Your linked Google or Facebook account must have 2-step verification enabled.

Account linking requirement

LootBar self top-up currently supports only accounts linked directly to Google or Facebook.

Can I use LootBar self top-up if my account is linked through Google Play Games?

No. Accounts linked through Google Play Games cannot use LootBar self top-up. At the moment, only accounts linked directly to Google or Facebook are supported.

Fast check: If your Kingshot account uses direct Google or Facebook login and that login already asks for an extra verification step, you are probably ready to continue.

Why 2-step verification is required

LootBar self top-up includes a security check during login.

Because of that, your linked Google or Facebook account should have 2-step verification enabled before you try to complete payment.

How to enable Google 2-step verification

  • Open your Google Account management page.
  • Go to the Security menu.
  • Find 2-Step Verification.
  • Choose a verification method such as a phone number or authenticator app.
  • Finish setup and confirm that it is active.

How to enable Facebook 2-step verification

  • Open Settings & Privacy in Facebook.
  • Go to Password and Security.
  • Find the two-factor authentication setting.
  • Choose a verification method such as SMS or an authenticator app.
  • Finish setup and confirm that it is active.

How to check whether 2-step verification is already enabled

The simplest way is to test your login in incognito mode.

  • Open Chrome or your browser in incognito mode.
  • Go to the Google login page or the Facebook login page.
  • Sign in with the same account linked to Kingshot.
  • If you receive an extra verification request by phone, email, or authenticator app, 2-step verification is active.
Check result: If you see phone verification, email verification, or an authenticator app approval request, your security verification is turned on and working.

How to top up step by step

Once your login method and 2-step verification are confirmed, the rest of the process is usually straightforward. Follow the steps below in order.

Top-up page

Open the LootBar Kingshot top-up page

Enter #s4qU in the search bar to apply an extra coupon before checkout.

Good news: If your account is ready, most users can finish self top-up just by following the steps below.

1. Choose one package

Select the package you want and click Top-up Now.

2. Add multiple packages if needed

If you want more than one package, use the cart button and add the items you need.

3. Continue to payment

Select your payment method, such as card or PayPal, and then click Pay Now.

The left option is self top-up and the right option is operator top-up. Be sure to select self top-up.

4. Sign in with your linked account

After selecting self top-up, the login screen will appear. For most users, Google login is the easiest option.

  • Enter your Google email address.
  • Enter your password.
Tip: If you are not sure about your password, confirm it first before continuing. This can save time during payment.

5. Complete cart payment

Review the total amount in your cart and finish payment.

6. Confirm completion

After payment is approved, the final confirmation screen will appear. Press Got it if needed.

Your top-up should now be completed.

7. Check your game account

Open Kingshot and confirm that the purchased package has been delivered correctly.

Frequently asked questions

Why does self top-up fail for some users?

Most failures are caused by the wrong login type or missing 2-step verification, not by the payment step itself.

Is Google Play Games supported?

No. Only accounts linked directly to Google or Facebook are supported for LootBar self top-up.

How do I know whether 2-step verification is active?

Try signing in through an incognito browser window. If you get an extra phone, email, or authenticator check, it is active.

Where do I enter the coupon?

Enter #s4qU in the search bar on LootBar before checkout so the extra coupon can be applied.

What should I do if I still get stuck?

Double-check your login method, verify that 2-step verification is active, and then ask in the Discord help channel if you still need support.
