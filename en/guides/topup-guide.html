Please check these first before using self top-up

Important: Please manage your own personal information and account security carefully. Before you start self top-up, make sure the following 2 conditions are met.
  • Your Kingshot account must be linked to Google or Facebook.
  • Your linked Google or Facebook account must have 2-step verification enabled.

This is explained first because most self top-up issues happen not because of the payment flow itself, but because of account linking status or missing 2-step verification.

Account linking requirement

LootBar self top-up is currently available only for accounts directly linked to Google or Facebook.

Q. My account is linked through Google Play Games.

Yes. Accounts linked through Google Play Games cannot use LootBar self top-up. At the moment, only accounts linked directly to Google or Facebook are supported.

Why Google / Facebook 2-step verification is required

LootBar self top-up requires a security check during login.

That is why your linked Google or Facebook account must have 2-step verification enabled before you can proceed normally.

How to enable Google 2-step verification

  • Open your Google Account management page.
  • Go to the Security menu.
  • Find the 2-Step Verification section.
  • Set up your preferred verification method, such as a phone number or authenticator app.
  • After setup, confirm that it has been applied correctly.

How to enable Facebook 2-step verification

  • Open Facebook Settings & Privacy.
  • Go to Password and Security.
  • Find the two-factor authentication setting.
  • Set up your preferred verification method, such as SMS or an authenticator app.
  • After setup, confirm that it has been applied correctly.

How to check whether 2-step verification is enabled

The easiest way is to try logging in through incognito mode.

  • Open Chrome or your browser in incognito mode.
  • Go to the Google login page or the Facebook login page.
  • Try signing in with your account.
  • If you receive an extra verification request by phone, email, or authenticator app, that account already has 2-step verification enabled.
Check result: If you see phone verification, email verification, or an authenticator app approval request, then 2-step verification is active.
