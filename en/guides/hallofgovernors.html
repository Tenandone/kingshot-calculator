Kingshot Hall of Governors Guide

A practical guide covering run 1–5 stage score tables, season hero unlock structure for Amadeus and Hilde, Lord Gear and Charm scores, and real progression tips in one place.

One-Line Summary

  • Hall of Governors is usually a biweekly event, but timing and structure can vary by server.
  • Amadeus in Season 1 is often not a simple milestone reward. In many cases, total ranking is the real requirement.
  • The most efficient way to score high is to stockpile speedups, shards, hammers, and roulette resources, then spend them only on the right stages.

Common Misunderstanding

Many players think finishing all event missions is enough to get Amadeus, but in Season 1 reward structures, ranking requirements such as TOP10 are often the real key.

If You Want Amadeus in Season 1

If your goal is to get Amadeus from Season 1 Hall of Governors rewards, then on many servers spending is close to mandatory. On competitive servers, speedups, shards, and roulette resources alone are often not enough to reach TOP10.

If you want a more reliable shot at Amadeus, it is better to line up your top-up timing with Hall of Governors.

1) Event Overview · Timing · Season Heroes

Hall of Governors is one of the main growth events in Kingshot and is usually held on a two-week cycle. During the event, you complete stage-specific tasks to earn points and claim milestone rewards from your total score.

  • Run 1: Starts on Day 6 after kingdom creation (server 기준). Season hero: Amadeus
  • Run 2: About two weeks after Run 1. Season hero: Amadeus
  • Run 3: Usually in the Generation 2 period, though some servers may still treat it differently. Season hero: Amadeus, usually 7 days long
  • Runs 4–5: Similar stage structure. Season hero: Hilde, usually tied to TOP100 ranking rewards

Note

Stage order, score values, and reward structure can change depending on server, version, and live operations. Always treat in-game notices as the final source of truth.

2) How Scoring Works (Summary)

Main scoring categories usually include the following.

  • Building upgrades (construction power gain)
  • Research upgrades (research power gain)
  • Troop training / promotion (troop power gain)
  • Using speedups (depending on run and stage)
  • Hero promotion (using shards)
  • Hero gear hammers / gear XP / exclusive gear materials
  • Hero roulette spins
  • Lord Gear score increase
  • Lord Charm score increase

6) Season Hero Unlock Conditions (TOP10 / TOP100)

Amadeus (Season 1) Unlock

Amadeus is often not awarded automatically just for clearing milestones. In many Season 1 formats, you need to place in a ranking bracket such as TOP10 to unlock him.

Hilde (Runs 4–5) Reward

In Runs 4–5, Hilde appears as the season hero, and her shards are often tied to TOP100 ranking rewards.

Exact score cutoffs and reward structure can differ by server, so always check the in-game notice for your own kingdom.

7) Q&A + F2P / Spender Tips

Hall of Governors Q&A Summary

Q1. When does Hall of Governors happen?

A. It often appears after Alliance Mobilization and usually returns on a roughly two-week cycle. Actual timing can vary by server, so always check announcements first.

Q2. Who can participate?

A. All governors on the server participate automatically. Your event score during the period determines your ranking and rewards.

Q3. How do you earn points?

A. Through troop training, building upgrades, research, speedup usage, hero promotion, hammers, roulette, and Lord Gear / Charm score increases. The exact valid categories depend on the current stage.

Q4. How should you prepare for high scores?

  • Save speedups and resources in advance, then spend them only when the right stage opens.
  • Your efficiency gets better when troop or research growth events overlap with Hall of Governors.
  • It helps to prepare construction, training, and research speedups, gear hammers, and enough diamonds or tickets for roulette ahead of time.

Q5. What rewards do you get?

A. Rewards usually include diamonds, speedups, and hero shards based on ranking. Lord Gear materials are often not included as direct ranking rewards.

Q6. How many points do you need for TOP10?

A. It varies by server, but TOP10 cutoffs are often around 500M points or higher. Competition is usually much harsher on newer servers.

Q7. Do you always need to push for top ranks?

A. Not always. Outside of important windows such as Season 1 Amadeus, later-generation heroes can often be upgraded with universal shards, so taking stable rewards may be more efficient than forcing a rank push.

Q8. How many diamonds should you prepare?

A. Since Hall of Governors often overlaps with Hero Roulette, having at least 162,000 diamonds prepared makes planning much easier.

If You Plan to Spend

If you are already willing to spend, then Season 1 Amadeus is usually worth prioritizing. For players investing hard early, getting Amadeus to a strong star level quickly can create a real advantage through the Season 1 to Season 2 period.

In particular, if you want a realistic shot at Season 1 TOP10, stockpiled resources alone are often not enough, so planning your top-up timing around Hall of Governors matters a lot.

F2P Core Advice

If you are F2P or light-spend, securing the roulette hero each generation matters a lot. Missing roulette heroes can leave you behind even against well-built low-spend accounts in arena and combat.
