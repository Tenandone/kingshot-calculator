Quick Summary

  • Coupons are sent on the 15th of every month.
  • Package coupons are sent to your coupon box at the beginning of the next month based on your monthly cumulative top-up amount.
  • You must register and top up through the promo link for your purchases to be tracked automatically.
  • LootBar automatically tracks your payments for the month and sends eligible rewards directly to your coupon box.
  • New users can enter #s4qU in the LootBar search bar to receive an extra coupon.
Note: Discount coupons and winning package rewards are issued directly by LootBar, and the reward screen will show the KingshotData logo.

Reward Rules

Extra package coupons are sent based on your monthly cumulative top-up amount under the following rules.

$500+ $4.99 Coupon

Among users with a monthly cumulative top-up of $500 or more, 20 users will receive this reward.

Higher-tier winners are excluded from this level.

$1,000+ $9.99 Coupon

Among users with a monthly cumulative top-up of $1,000 or more, 10 users will receive this reward.

The top 3 cumulative top-up users are excluded from this level.

$2,000+ $99.99 Coupon

Among users with a monthly cumulative top-up of $2,000 or more, the top 3 cumulative top-up users will receive this reward.

The reward is sent automatically at the beginning of the next month.

Delivery schedule: Discount coupons are sent on the 15th of each month, and package coupons are sent automatically to your coupon box at the beginning of the next month.

FAQ

Q. It is already the start of the month, but I still have not received my coupon or package reward. Why?

Discount coupons are sent on the 15th of each month, and package coupons are sent automatically at the beginning of the next month. Also, only payments completed through the promo link will be counted.

Q. Can I use the dedicated link on mobile or PC?

Yes. You can use it on both mobile and PC. It is a good idea to bookmark the link so you do not have to search for it every time.

Q. Which link do I need to use for this to count?

Q. Is there an extra reward for new users too?

Yes. New users can enter #s4qU in the LootBar search bar after signing up to receive an extra coupon. However, to have your extra rewards tracked automatically, you still need to register and top up through the promo link.
